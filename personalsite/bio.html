About Meg

Welcome to Meg's Lil Soul Patches! I'm Megan Grubaugh, the creative mind behind these delightful stickers. Each design is crafted with love and inspired by the whimsical and soulful moments in life. Whether you're looking for something cute, quirky, or heartfelt, my stickers are here to add a spark of joy to your day!

When I'm not designing stickers, I love exploring nature, drinking copious amounts of hot chocolate, and cuddling with my cats, Stormy and Shadow. Thank you for stopping by, and I hope you find a sticker that speaks to your soul!
